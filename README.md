# 🌍 Air Quality Action

An AI-powered web application that helps users understand Air Quality Index (AQI) data in simple language and provides personalized recommendations to reduce the impact of air pollution. The project combines a responsive website with IBM watsonx Orchestrate to deliver an interactive AI assistant for air quality awareness.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Air pollution is a major public health concern that affects millions of people worldwide. Although Air Quality Index (AQI) data is widely available, it is often presented in technical formats that are difficult for the general public to understand. Most existing systems only display pollution levels and do not provide personalized guidance or actionable recommendations.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

Air Quality Action is an intelligent web application that converts complex AQI information into simple, user-friendly insights. It enables users to understand air quality levels, learn about common air pollutants, and receive personalized health recommendations through an AI-powered assistant integrated with IBM watsonx Orchestrate.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🌍 Responsive single-page website
- 🤖 AI-powered chatbot using IBM watsonx Orchestrate
- 📊 AQI Checker with instant air quality status
- 📖 Easy-to-understand AQI categories
- 🌫️ Information about major air pollutants
- 💬 Personalized air quality guidance
